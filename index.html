Information

Soul Chain is a ZERO game-engine 3D FPS Retro style shooter, featuring various weapons and enemies with unlimited proceduarly generated levels/rooms to clear of demoic hordes! Kill enemies and collect their souls in order to utilize advanced abilities and escape HELL!




    Technology

  • Infinite proceduarly generated rooms to clear of incoming enemy demon hordes
  • Dynamic particle system builder streamlines creating custom effects to use in-game
  • Smart AI pathfinding utilizing A* with line of sight path skipping and smoothing
  • Custom shader and render pipeline structure enhances the look and feel of the game
  • Updating mini-map with the level layout including Player and Enemy icons
  • Decal projection system allows for a responsive & dynamic ever-changing game world
  • A built in level editor lets you create and test custom built rooms and challenges
  • Raycast hit detection for the environment and enemies
  • Scalable enemy spawn rates allows for the management of level/room difficulty
